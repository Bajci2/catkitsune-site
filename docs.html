🚀 CatKitSune Launchpad

Fast. Curated. Trusted. — A curated NFT launchpad built on Hyperliquid (HyperEVM). We help projects launch, gain traction, and get listed.

🧩

What is CatKitSune?

CatKitSune is not a marketplace. We are a launchpad designed to help new NFT projects on Hyperliquid.

  • 🚀 Launch their collection on Hyperliquid
  • 👁️ Gain visibility in the Hyperliquid ecosystem
  • 📋 Get listed on platforms like DripTrade or OpenSea

We focus on curated, high-quality projects only.

⚙️

How it works

1
Submit your project Send us your collection details via Twitter or Discord
2
Review process (48–72h) We review your project and get back to you
3
Launch & mint on our platform Your collection goes live on catkitsune.xyz
4
Get listed on DripTrade / OpenSea We forward approved projects for trading
